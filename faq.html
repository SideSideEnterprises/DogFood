Below are all the questions and their answers

1 . Why are fresh and local so important?

The average dog treat you find on store shelves has a shelf life of 6 months to 3 and a half years and spent several months of its life in a warehouse far, far away from your hometown… how??? Dog treat companies use chemical preservatives to elongate the shelf life of their treats. These preservatives prevent mold and spoiling but result in treats with a lower level of nutritional value. At our local commercial kitchen, our treats are baked and delivered within 10 days of receiving your order. We use no preservatives, utilizing only our low heat baking process and oxygen packs to ensure a shelf life of 2-3 months when properly stored. Doing this allows us to bake better tasting and more nutritional treats than any treats you’ll find in large retail stores or large online retailers.

2 . Why is quality so important?

The dog food and dog treat industry was born on the idea of using the by-products, also known as the waste, from human meat consumption for our furry friends. In recent years, this has received much negative publicity all throughout the country. Many major producers still use by-products to maintain their bottom line. We chose to be different. Our chicken comes from all white meat chicken breast, the same chicken you or I would eat. Our beef is the same beef restaurants use for burgers. Our dogs are family, their food and treats should be made from the same quality ingredients as any other family member! That is why every ingredient we use is purchased from grocery stores and always human grade.

3 . How are Saint Rocco’s Treats made?

For an interactive explanation of our process feel free to visit the “our process” page of our website! We begin with our limited ingredient, human grade recipes. From there, we mix the recipe together and hand press the mix onto our baking pans. We bake the panned mix for 1 hour and after cooling, hand slice the loaves into strips! After hand slicing, our treats enter our dehydrator for a low and slow cook to ensure we maintain their nutritional value and also ensure they don’t soil too quickly. To finish the process off we package our treats and hit the roads on our way to all our furry friends' homes.

4 . What ingredients are used in Saint Rocco’s Treats?

For an interactive explanation of every ingredient we use, plus their benefits, feel free to visit the “our ingredients” page of our website!

5 . How should I store my Saint Rocco’s Treats?

At Saint Rocco’s Treats we use no chemical preservatives and all human grade ingredients. This means the storage of our treats is important. Be sure to store our treats in a dry, room temperature storage location (For example, a pantry, laundry room or kitchen counter). Keeping our treats away from too much direct, high temperatures and/or moisture is best to prevent molding or soiling. After each use it is also very important to reseal our bags. Taking all of these precautions will ensure our treats outlast your pup’s cravings for them!

6 . How long do Saint Rocco’s Treats stay fresh?

Our treats have a minimum shelf life of 10-12 weeks when all the correct storage precautions are taken. On each bag, our “best by” date is not when the treats go bad but rather a date to gauge how long you have had your treats. If you still have your bag of treats 6 weeks after the best by date has passed it is best to place them in the freezer to elongate their life or purchase a new bag. If you ever notice any discoloration, mold or soiling of our treats, never hesitate to contact us and we’ll get you a new bag free of charge as soon as we can!

7 . How many treats should I feed my dog?

Feeding instructions for our treats are dependent upon the weight of your dog. You must ALWAYS feed our treats as exactly that, a treat. They should not be fed as a meal and should never exceed 10% of your dogs daily diet. Our treats are high in protein which is excellent for muscle growth and energy but too much protein can lead to fat. Always keep your dog active and ensure they have plenty of water when you are feeding treats!

Below is our lab approved guide to ensure you are always feeding your furry baby the right way!


5 to 10lb dog: 1 strip or 2 cubes a day

Based on: Our treats being 5% of the recommended 125 to 300 calorie diet per day to maintain the weight of a dog this size.

11 to 25lb dog: 1 strip or 3 cubes a day

Based on: Our treats being 5% of the recommended 300 to 750 calorie diet per day to maintain the weight of a dog this size.

26 to 35lb dog: 2 strip or 4 cubes a day

Based on: Our treats being 5% of the recommended 780 to 1050 calorie diet per day to maintain the weight of a dog this size.

36 to 50lb dog: 2 strip or 5 cubes a day

Based on: Our treats being 5% of the recommended 1080 to 1500 calorie diet per day to maintain the weight of a dog this size.

50+lb dog: 2 strip or 7 cubes a day

Based on: Our treats being 5% of the recommended 1500+ calorie diet per day to maintain the weight of a dog this size.
Add 1 cube every 15lbs and 1 strips every 30lbs

8 . How does shipping work?

We offer 2 options for shipping. The first is local Bucks County, PA delivery. All furry friends within 35-40 minutes of our local kitchen will receive access to this option. Your order will be delivered to your front porch by one of our delivery drivers every Friday! The second option, all furry friends outside of the aforementioned range will receive their order via UPS shipment. We deliver to our local UPS every Friday and our treats will reach your home within 3-5 days after our Friday delivery!

9 . How long does it take orders to be delivered to my home?

Our orders are always delivered within 2 to 10 business days. Whether you receive access to Bucks County, PA delivery or UPS shipping your treats will always be delivered within this range. If our treats have not been delivered within this range please contact us and we will find out where your treats are immediately!

10 . I forgot my password, can I reset it?

Yes! It is very easy to reset your password. Press “Forgot Password”, provide your email and go to your email inbox where you will now have the opportunity to reset your password and sign back into your Saint Rocco’s Treats account!

10 . What if my dog doesn’t love Saint Rocco’s Treats?

You have two options. Either pick another flavor and we’ll deliver it to your home free of charge or receive a 100% refund! We doubt we’ll ever have this problem but even delicious dog treats need insurance right?

10 . Where do Saint Rocco’s Treats donations go?

This month and every month since July 2020 our donations have been going to Phoenix Assistance Dogs, or PAD for short. PAD is a local non-profit dog rescue located in Reading, PA. You can learn more about PAD on the “Our Mission” page of our website. PAD rescues dogs and trains them to become service dogs for those with disabilities. It is an amazing cause, run by even better people and we couldn’t feel more lucky to help where we can.
